About Us

Mason Hack Club is a computer programming club at William Mason High School in Mason, Ohio, and is a chapter of the international Hack Club organization. We meet on Tuesdays in room Z211.

  • learn more languages
  • volunteer locally
  • showcase projects
  • connect with companies
